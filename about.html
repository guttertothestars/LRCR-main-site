Little Rome is, first and foremost, a family endeavor that was born when my wife and I lost childcare during COVID. Freelancing gave me the freedom to have a flexible schedule and give my daughter the attention she deserves. ßThat's what I get out of it.

I have spent nearly a decade in leadership roles at two, well-known shops in Washington, DC. However, my love for bicycles started in the unlikeliest of cities: my hometown, the car-centric Houston. The spark for bicycle repair began when I got a flat four miles from home (in the Texas July heat!). When I moved to DC, I joined the team at The Bike Rack in Logan Circle as the Service Manager.

At The Bike Rack, major accomplishments included managing up to twelve fellow mechanics, supporting the opening of a second location, redesigning the interior layout to reflect industry trends towards repair-focused shops, and maximizing revenue while developing close relationships with loyal customers. Many of these individuals made Little Rome possible. Just before COVID, I was the Service Manager at Conte's Bike Shop, a full-service, multi-city bike retailer that took over The Bike Rack’s Logan Circle location.

Throughout the years, I have seen it all, from pre-war, three speeds to World-Tour level race machines — sometimes one right after the other. I believe that bicycles transform people’s lives. My interests are reliable wheel builds, frame up custom builds, and maintenance of high-mileage bicycles for people who love to ride. I am a member of the Professional Bicycle Mechanics Association and Shimano S-TEC certified.

Now, the team includes my toddler who knows that Papa “loves fixing things,” my cats who may occasionally sniff your wheels, and my wife who pitches in as a wordsmith. Little Rome allows me to do the work that made me fall in love with being a mechanic: uninterrupted, unhurried, and tailored to your needs. And the name is a nod to Brookland, the DC neighborhood where we live.

To sum up, I get to be your bicycle’s advocate. That's what you get out of it.

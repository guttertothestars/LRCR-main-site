Bicyle Repair Services

Little Rome is dedicated to creating the best possible repair experience in DC. Little Rome is service - it is all we do. The rates are competitive and the work is guaranteed. Services are done by appointment and pickup and dropoff may be available.

Every repair starts with a thoughtful dialogue and a comprehensive assessment of your bike's needs. Transparency and honesty are core tenets. The only thing we have to sell is your bike in its best form. We aim to be your bike's best advocate.

Rough Pricing Guidelines

Not every bike repair falls into a neat category and often a repair estimate will be done with an a la carte collection of needs.

If a bike does need a tune up there are three broad packages that are $100, $200, and $300. The nuts and bolts (and cables and chains) of each package can be delved into if your bike needs one. Most heavily-ridden bikes could use the middle package annually-ish. If you do get a tuneup you'll get a copy of a very detailed checklist of checks and repairs, as well as notes about your job along with guidance for the future.

For repairs that don't fall into a package rates are competitive for the DC market - about what you would expect from any shop you walked into.

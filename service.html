Tune-Up Packages

Aedile (Basic) Tune: $145

A good choice if your bicycle is in good shape but needs a little love. Complete safety check of all components and structures as well as precision adjustments to shifting, brake, steering, and wheel truing and hub adjustments. Minor cleaning to help extend the life of the components and improve overall performance. All bolts are torque checked for safety. Free install of tires and tubes. Installation of other parts 50% off.

Praetor (Upgrade) Tune: $225

For dedicated all-weather commuters or high-mileage, race bicycles. The drivetrain gets a luxury spa bath for maximum shine. Everything is adjusted —shifting, braking, hubs, wheels, headset, and bottom bracket. All bolts are torque checked for safety. This package includes all parts installation costs, including brake bleeds.
